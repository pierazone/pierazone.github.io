Our Goal

This has been created to ease the educator’s task of conducting exams by adding convenient features and none unnecessary. We created this during the pandemic (COVID-19) times where it has become a real trouble for educators to conduct exams remotely or in place but with lesser vigilance, this triggered the idea of creating a software that is free to use by all and most importantly is simple and easy to use, while still having a means that could prevent malpractices. This was definitely a tasking process as we had to find time from our regular study schedule to work on this, it may not be perfect yet but if serves its task well for sure. It would mean a ton to us if somebody is actually benefited by this, as that would justify our time and efforts. We would love to hear your thoughts, please visit the contact page to write to us or visit the reviews page to write a public review and let others know.

As we are running on a zero-budget project, we need the support of the community to spread a word about it and keep improving to serve you better.
